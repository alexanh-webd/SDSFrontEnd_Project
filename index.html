PG UNLEASHED 1/60 RX-93 nu Gundam

The PGU Nu Gundam is a 1/60 scale Perfect Grade Unleashed model kit of the RX-93 Nu Gundam, featuring advanced articulation, detailed internal framing, and full psycommu weaponry recreation.

The PGU Nu Gundam is part of Bandai's Perfect Grade Unleashed (PGU) line, celebrating the 45th anniversary of Gunpla. It is a 1/60 scale model of the RX-93 Nu Gundam, the iconic mobile suit piloted by Amuro Ray in Mobile Suit Gundam: Char’s Counterattack. This model kit represents the pinnacle of Gunpla engineering, offering a highly detailed internal frame and external armor that faithfully reproduces the original mobile suit's design.

Details of MGEX Gunpla

MGEX 1/100 UNICORN GUNDAM VER. KA

The MGEX 1/100 Unicorn Gundam Ver.Ka is a high-end model kit that celebrates the 40th anniversary of Gunpla. It features an innovative LED light system that illuminates the iconic psycho frame with both red and green glows, showcasing the Unicorn Gundam's unique transformable structure. The kit includes a flexible LED sheet for easy assembly and space-saving design, allowing for the first Unicorn Gundam to transform, light up, change colors, and articulate at the same time.

The entire form and proportions have been reconstructed under Mr. Hajime Katoki’s full supervision, and it comes with a dedicated MS cage that stores the battery box of the lighting unit and holds the weapon. Three AA alkaline batteries are required to recreate the light-up effects.

MGEX 1/100 Strike Freedom Gundam

The MGEX 1/100 Strike Freedom Gundam is a high-end model kit from Bandai SPIRITS, known for its extreme expression in bringing Mobile Suit Gundam to life. This kit is themed around the highest-peak metal expression in Gunpla history, featuring six distinct patterns, three special surface treatments, two metallic molding colors, and a sheet of etched markings.

The internal frame is equipped with the largest number of frame-linked motion gimmicks in MG history, and it includes a wide variety of optional weapons. The kit is available for purchase through various online platforms, including the official Bandai Hobby site and other hobby shops.

What do you mean by adding Plaplate? What is it?

Pla plates, short for plastic plates, is the term generally used by gunpla modelers to refer to what’s commonly known as styrene sheeting. Styrene, a type of plastic, is a preferred material produced for modelers looking to scratch build or customize their projects. It’s fairly common and applies to many hobbies, most commonly model trains, making it quite common in hobby shops. In the United States many shops feature a shelf with plates from Evergreen Scale Models or Plastruct.
